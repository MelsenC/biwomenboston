About

Welcome to Bi Women Boston, the home of the Boston Bisexual Women’s Network and the Bi Women Quarterly, produced in Boston for women everywhere.

This site is an affirming space for women with bi+ (bi, pan, fluid, and other non-binary sexualities) and those who love and support us.

Write

Spring 2018: Chosen Family

Chosen families are groups of people who deliberately choose to play significant roles in each other’s lives. Who makes up your chosen family? How did you come to find each other? What does your chosen family mean to you and your bi+ identity?

Submissions due: February 1

Summer 2018: Bodies

Let’s talk about our bodies: Body image. Gendered bodies. Bodies and health. Changing bodies. Bodies converging and merging. Bodies and attraction. Sexuality and the body. We welcome your personal stories, poetry, artwork, or opinion pieces.

Submissions due: May 1

Open Call for Submissions

We welcome news articles about current issues and events, creative writing, musings, book reviews, calendar listings, letters to the editor, visual art (photos and drawings), poetry, and more.
