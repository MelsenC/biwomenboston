Folks, I am so excited! We just finished designing & producing several new pins on our wonderful high-quality pin-making machine.

All proceeds from sales support the production of the Bi Women Quarterly. 

Prices (including postage, within the United States):
$3 each
$8 for 3 pins
$12 for 6 pins
$30 for 20 pins

If you are outside the United States and wish to order pins, please contact me at biwomeneditor@gmail.com.

You can order via Paypal to biwomenboston@gmail.com, or by check to BBWN, POB 391727, Jamaica Plain MA 02130. Please specify (by number or words) which pin(s) you would like. It may take up to two weeks to process your order, especially if Robyn is on tour, as she is the one who takes care of this.
